General Concerns & Issues

Standard procedures and responses for common customer concerns and issues.

Common Issues

Below are the most frequently encountered general concerns and their standard resolutions:

  1. Missing Packages
    • Check tracking status in system
    • Verify delivery address
    • Contact local facility
    • Initiate trace process if needed
  2. Delayed Shipments
    • Check weather conditions
    • Verify customs clearance status
    • Confirm operational delays
    • Provide updated ETA
  3. Damaged Goods
    • Request photos of damage
    • Initiate claim process
    • Arrange for inspection
    • Process replacement/refund
  4. Billing Disputes
    • Review invoice details
    • Verify service charges
    • Check for discounts applied
    • Adjust invoice if warranted
  5. Address Corrections
    • Verify correct address format
    • Update delivery instructions
    • Reroute if in transit
    • Charge address correction fee
  6. Pickup Issues
    • Confirm pickup window
    • Verify package readiness
    • Check driver availability
    • Reschedule if necessary
  7. International Customs
    • Verify documentation complete
    • Check duty/tax calculations
    • Contact customs broker
    • Provide clearance updates
  8. Account Access
    • Reset password if needed
    • Verify user permissions
    • Update contact information
    • Enable/disable features
  9. Service Complaints
    • Document complaint details
    • Escalate to supervisor
    • Follow up with resolution
    • Offer compensation if appropriate
  10. Technical Issues
    • Clear browser cache/cookies
    • Try different browser
    • Check system status page
    • Report to IT if ongoing

Escalation Procedures

Follow these steps for issue escalation:

Level 1: Customer Service Representative - Attempt initial resolution - Document all interactions - Set follow-up timeline Level 2: Senior Representative/Supervisor - Review case details - Contact customer directly - Offer alternative solutions Level 3: Department Manager - Investigate root cause - Implement process improvements - Authorize compensation Level 4: Operations Director - Strategic issue resolution - Cross-department coordination - Policy review/update

Response Time Standards

Issue Type Initial Response Resolution Target Escalation Point
Urgent/Critical 1 hour 4 hours 2 hours no progress
High Priority 4 hours 24 hours 8 hours no progress
Standard 8 hours 48 hours 24 hours no progress
Low Priority 24 hours 5 business days 3 days no progress

Documentation Requirements

All concerns must include:

  • Customer name and account number
  • Date/time of incident
  • Tracking/reference numbers
  • Detailed description
  • Steps taken so far
  • Customer communication history
  • Supporting documents/files
  • Proposed resolution
